Financial Investment Schemes

Public Provident Fund (PPF)

Long-term savings scheme with tax benefits under Section 80C. Offers interest rates determined by government quarterly.

  • Interest Rate: 7.1% p.a. (variable)
  • Lock-in Period: 15 years
  • Min Investment: ₹500 per year
  • Max Investment: ₹1.5 lakh per year
Learn More

Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana

Small savings scheme for girl child education and marriage expenses with tax benefits.

  • Interest Rate: 7.6% p.a. (variable)
  • Eligibility: Parents of girl child below 10 years
  • Min Investment: ₹250 per year
  • Max Investment: ₹1.5 lakh per year
Learn More

National Pension System (NPS)

Voluntary retirement savings scheme to enable systematic savings for retirement.

  • Returns: Market-linked
  • Tax Benefits: Under Section 80C and 80CCD
  • Min Contribution: ₹500 per month
  • Withdrawal: Partial allowed at 60 years
Learn More

Andhra Pradesh Government Schemes

Jagananna Vidya Deevena

Full reimbursement of college fees for eligible students pursuing higher education.

  • Eligibility: Students from families with annual income below ₹2.5 lakh
  • Benefit: 100% fee reimbursement
  • Disbursement: Quarterly basis
Learn More

YSR Cheyutha

Financial assistance to women of SC, ST, BC, and Minority communities aged 45-60 years.

  • Eligibility: Women aged 45-60 from SC, ST, BC, Minority communities
  • Benefit: ₹75,000 over 4 years (₹18,750 per annum)
  • Purpose: Economic empowerment and self-reliance
Learn More

YSR Rythu Bharosa

Financial assistance to farmer families to support agricultural investment.

  • Eligibility: All farmer families including tenants
  • Benefit: ₹13,500 per year
  • Disbursement: Three installments per year
Learn More
